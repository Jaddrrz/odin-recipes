Hamburger

Description

A patty or burger (in British English) is a flattened, usually round, serving of ground meat and/or legumes, grains, vegetables, or meat alternatives. Patties are found in multiple cuisines throughout the world.

Ingredients

  • Defrosted burger ready to cook
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ariosto spice (optional)
  • Egg (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat a grill and sprinkle with salt
  2. Put the burger/s on the grill
  3. Sprinkle with salt and spice of your choice
  4. Check the cooking on the bottom and flip if you see burnt lines
  5. Repeat the last step 2 more times
