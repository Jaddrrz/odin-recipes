• Defrosted burger ready to cook
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ariosto spice (optional)
  • Egg (optional)
  1. Heat a grill and sprinkle with salt
  2. Put the burger/s on the grill
  3. Sprinkle with salt and spice of your choice
  4. Check the cooking on the bottom and flip if you see burnt lines
  5. Repeat the last step 2 more times
