• Valeriana Salad
  • Tomatoes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  1. Pour the Valeriana in a relatively big bowl
  2. Chop the tomato into small and thin pieces
  3. Pour 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  4. Add extra-virgin olive oil and salt to your liking
  5. Start mixing until it tastes good
