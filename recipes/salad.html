My Salad

Description

A salad is a dish consisting of mixed ingredients, frequently vegetables. They are typically served chilled or at room temperature, though some can be served warm. Condiments and salad dressings, which exist in a variety of flavors, are often used to enhance a salad.

Ingredients

  • Valeriana Salad
  • Tomatoes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour the Valeriana in a relatively big bowl
  2. Chop the tomato into small and thin pieces
  3. Pour 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  4. Add extra-virgin olive oil and salt to your liking
  5. Start mixing until it tastes good
